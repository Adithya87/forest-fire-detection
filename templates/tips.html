Fire Prevention Tips

  • Clear dry vegetation and leaves from around your home.
  • Maintain a defensible space of 30 feet around structures.
  • Do not burn trash during dry, windy conditions.
  • Install spark arresters on chimneys and stovepipes.
  • Report any suspicious activity in forested areas to authorities.
